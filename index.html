Future Museum Exhibit

A speculative exploration of archaeological futurism

In the future, photogrammetric algorithmic tools will stitch together digital images to re-present objects, buildings and other artifacts that no longer exist physically. We will excavate our massive collection of digital images to recreate spaces and ideas of the past. We will combine these re-fabricated images with primary documents to gain an understanding of how things were.
